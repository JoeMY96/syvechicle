About us

Who we are

Established in 2013, our company has spent over a decade solidifying its position as a pioneering force in the electric vehicle industry. We specialize in crafting a comprehensive range of electric vehicles, including electric tricycles, golf carts, tuk-tuks, and freight tricycles.

Our diverse product line caters to a wide array of needs. Electric tricycles offer a convenient and cost-effective choice for personal and commercial use. Tuk-tuks stand out with their unique charm, making them ideal for short-distance urban transportation, while freight tricycles are engineered to handle heavy loads with ease, ensuring efficient cargo transit. However, our technological prowess truly shines in our golf carts. Equipped with advanced autonomous driving technology, our golf carts can navigate golf courses and resorts independently, providing an unparalleled, convenient, and safe experience for users.

Quality, innovation, and performance define our products. Constructed from high-grade materials, they are energy-efficient, eco-friendly, and durable. Our robust R & D team constantly explores cutting-edge technologies, integrating features like smart connectivity and enhanced battery management systems. This allows us to not only maintain top-notch product quality but also customize solutions to meet each customer’s unique requirements.

Our vision is clear: to be the world’s go-to provider of sustainable and technologically advanced electric vehicle solutions. By driving innovation and promoting electric mobility, we aim to reshape the future of transportation and contribute to a greener planet.
